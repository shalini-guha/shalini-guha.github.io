The Event

Optronix 2017 is an international scientific conference due to be held on 2-3 November, 2017 at University of Engineering and Management, Kolkata.

What Optronix is All About

We are proud to present Optronix, 2017, a conference that showcases and highlights recent trends of research and development in the area of Opto-electronics with particular emphasis on communication engineering and its different kinds.

The primary purpose of this conference is to raise awareness about the domain among established and budding scientists and researchers in India and abroad, encourage the involvement of students and faculty in study, research and development in this field, and also to raise public knowledge and move swiftly and effectively towards a knowledge-centric society.
